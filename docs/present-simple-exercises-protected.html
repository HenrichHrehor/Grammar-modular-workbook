Present Simple Exercises

Practice affirmative, negative, and question forms

Exercise 1: Add -s/-es/-ies to the verb

Instructions: Write the correct form of the verb for "he/she/it".

1. She (play) _____________ tennis every Saturday.
2. He (watch) _____________ TV in the evening.
3. My brother (study) _____________ English at university.
4. The train (go) _____________ to London every morning.
5. She (do) _____________ her homework after school.

Exercise 2: Complete the affirmative sentences

Instructions: Use the correct form of the verb in brackets.

1. I (like) _____________ chocolate ice cream.
2. My friends (live) _____________ in a big city.
3. The shop (open) _____________ at 9 o'clock.
4. We (speak) _____________ English in class.
5. My cat (sleep) _____________ on the sofa every day.

Exercise 3: Make negative sentences

Instructions: Use "do not" or "does not" + base verb.

1. I (like) spicy food.
→ I _____________________ spicy food.
2. She (play) basketball.
→ She _____________________ basketball.
3. They (work) on Sundays.
→ They _____________________ on Sundays.
4. He (drink) coffee.
→ He _____________________ coffee.
5. My parents (watch) TV in the morning.
→ My parents _____________________ TV in the morning.

Exercise 4: Make questions

Instructions: Use "Do" or "Does" + subject + base verb.

1. You speak French.
→ _____________________ French?
2. She likes pizza.
→ _____________________ pizza?
3. They live in London.
→ _____________________ in London?
4. He works in a bank.
→ _____________________ in a bank?
5. Your brother studies medicine.
→ _____________________ medicine?
