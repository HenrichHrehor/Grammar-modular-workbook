Grammar appendix

Passive voice — active ↔ passive across tenses (be + past participle).

Present simple: They use the road. → The road is used.

Past simple: They built the road. → The road was built.

Present perfect: They have repaired it. → It has been repaired.

Future: They will finish it. → It will be finished.
